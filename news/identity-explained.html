Unlocking the Hidden Stories behind our branding

Hoa Sen Motorsports goes beyond a mere name; it embodies a rich philosophy that permeates every aspect of our organization, reflecting a deep appreciation for Vietnamese culture and its symbolism.

Rishikesh Dwivedi

November 6, 2023

The Name

As members of Hoa Sen Motorsports, we take immense pride in every aspect of our brand. The name, "Hoa Sen Motorsports", holds deep significance as it symbolises the lotus flower, a sacred and iconic plant in Vietnamese culture, representing purity, resilience, and enlightenment. It reflects our commitment to upholding these values in our pursuit of excellence on the racetrack.

Just like the lotus flower, Hoa Sen Motorsports aims to transcend limitations, overcome challenges, and rise to the top. We embody the qualities of resilience, adaptability, and unwavering determination, constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in motorsports.

The Logo

The white lotus logo signifies our commitment to the pursuit of speed, precision, and technical expertise, while also embodies our strong community among employees, fans, and partners.

The overlapping petal design not only mimics accurately how the lotus flower look, but it also symbolises the unity of the Hoa Sen community. Within the big lotus, one can observe 3 additional lotus created by the overlap. Each of these lotuses represents a constituent part of Hoa Sen:

  • The most inner lotus is the team itself - the central core of Hoa Sen.
  • The second lotus is our partners - who we closely collaborate with.
  • The last lotus is the community - composing of fans, followers, and people interested.

All of these three smaller lotus is integrated with elegance into our logo, thereby showing the interconnectivity between each party.

The official Hoa Sen Motorsports logo. Source: Hoa Sen Motorsports

We also want to emphasise our high standards of technicality and pristine, therefore we have designed the logo with intricacy and based our inspiration behind a white lotus: a symbol of pristine in the Vietnamese culture.

The Slogan

Driven by Determination, Fuelled by Passion, United in Respect

—Hoa Sen Motorsports, a student F1 team

Our slogan is a powerful and impactful statement that encapsulates our core values and principles. It is designed to communicate the team's strong sense of purpose, motivation, and collaboration, capturing the essence of our aspirations:

  1. "Driven by Determination": This phrase highlights our relentless pursuit of goals and the unwavering commitment to success. It conveys a strong sense of focus, ambition, and the willingness to overcome obstacles and challenges along the way.
  2. "Fuelled with Passion": The work of Hoa Sen is not solely driven by external factors or obligations but by a genuine passion and genuine enjoyment for what we do. This part of the slogan emphasizes that intense enthusiasm, love, and dedication that the individuals within the team have for their craft or mission.
  3. "United in Respect": Despite differences in roles, backgrounds, or perspectives, everyone here recognizes and values each other's contributions. This last phrase conveys a sense of teamwork, cohesion, and the understanding that success is achieved through collective effort.

The Colours

The colours of our brand includes: Red (#da251d), Yellow (#edc967), White (#ffffff), and Black (#000000); each carrying with them symbolism which fully presents our background and core values.

The colour quartet: red, yellow, white, black that makes up Hoa Sen Motorsports' identity. Source: Hoa Sen Motorsports

  • Red — Red is a significant color in Vietnamese culture and symbolizes luck, happiness, and celebration. It also symbolises our burning passion and vibrant energy.
  • Yellow — Yellow adds a touch of opulence and sophistication to our branding, encompassing our goals of being elegant and prestige. It is also the colour of the star in the center of our country's flag.
  • Black — Black represents our brand's elegance and formality.
  • White — White serves mainly as the logo's colour; the white lotus represents purity, simplicity, and pristine.
